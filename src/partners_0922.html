Just in Time for Dreamforce 2022: Acqueon's Integration for Salesforce Service Cloud Voice

We’re at Dreamforce 2022 this week and Patrick Beyries, our Chief Product Officer (the driving force behind Acqueon’s product strategy, vision & a Salesforce alum), will be there. He'd love to connect this week or anytime! And just in time for #DF22, our brand-new outbound campaign manager integration for Salesforce Service Cloud Voice (SCV) is live!

Acqueon's integration for SCV is an out-of-the-box integration natively built into both Salesforce Service Cloud and Sales Cloud UI. Seamlessly layering into SCV, it enables advanced, high-volume outbound campaign management. It also offers user-friendly SCV desktop gadgets to support efficient setups and allow agents to handle blended voice interactions inside the desktop.

The Acqueon + SCV integration delivers users a best-in-class omnichannel (voice + digital), campaign, list management & dialer solution.

Acqueon Supports Both Service Cloud Voice Models

  • Support for Model 1: Service Cloud Voice with Amazon Connect (SCV pre-integrated w/ Amazon Connect)
  • Support for Model 2: Service Cloud Voice for Partner Telephony (Amazon Connect Telephony/Services purchased from Partner)

DISH Network: A Noble Replacement Success Story

How DISH Network Won by Upgrading to Acqueon

  • Who is DISH: A television & direct-broadcast satellite provider who had a bold idea — to bring TV to rural America, connecting millions of Americans to the beloved programs traditional cable companies wouldn’t. Today, they serve ~19 million customers!
  • Reason for DISH’s Upgrade from Noble to Acqueon: DISH needed an outbound campaign management upgrade because their Noble solution wasn't integrated into voice—meaning only email & digital outbound campaigns were possible.
  • WHY ACQUEON? Acqueon Engagement, our omnichannel campaign manager, fully integrated with DISH’s outbound autodialer, offered a huge increase in flexibility & customization versus Noble.The cherry on top was that Acqueon's platform-agnostic Campaign Manager integration didn't require DISH to switch or modify their existing contact center infrastructure. Once DISH made the switch to Acqueon Engagement, it was implemented & operational in under 6 months.
  • With our comprehensive omnichannel campaign manager, DISH Network can now execute 2x the campaigns!
