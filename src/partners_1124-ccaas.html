Acqueon Use Cases:
Help Your Customers Boost Revenue

We know one size doesn’t fit all, especially when it comes to sales. That’s why we tailor our solutions for each customer’s unique business needs. Whether they’re in retail, banking, healthcare, or another industry, your customers need modern sales and campaign management solutions that seamlessly integrate with their contact center.

Primary Use Cases We Support:

  • ⚡ Speed-to-Lead (Inbound Sales)
    Timely lead response and responding to all leads are important factors for minimizing cost per lead. By using digital plus voice cadences, all leads are responded to immediately. With an intelligent follow-up of cadences, leads are converted to wins at high rates, improving revenue per lead.
    Target Personas: Chief Sales Officer/Head of Sales, Campaign Admins, Chief Customer/Operating Officer, Contact Center Architects

  • 💲 Sales Campaigns (Outbound Sales)
    Effective campaign management helps your customers make the most of prospecting, member enrollment, or appointment scheduling campaigns.
    Target Personas: Chief Sales Officer/Head of Sales, Campaign Admins, Chief Customer/Operating Officer, Contact Center Architects

  • 🏷️ Sales and Appointment Reminders
    This use case is for sales and appointment-related reminders and the management of appointments. Additionally, in the healthcare industry, reminders play a critical role throughout both the member and patient journey including referrals, pre- and post-encounter activities, medication adherence, and prescription refills.
    Target Personas: Chief Sales Officer/Head of Sales, Campaign Admins, Chief Customer/Operating Officer, Contact Center Architects

  • 💳 Collections
    This use case is for pre-due date bill notifications and post-due date collection reminders for credit cards, mortgage payments, auto loans, and other loan products. For healthcare customers, this use case applies to medical debt and associated taxes.
    Target Personas: Director of Credit & Collections, Campaign Admins, Chief Customer/Operating Officer, Contact Center Architects
