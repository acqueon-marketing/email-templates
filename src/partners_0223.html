Product Update: New Compliance Module for Acqueon Engagement

Compliance for CFPB, FDCPA & Ofcom.

Your customers no longer have to worry about steep penalties for noncompliance, because we handle Compliance checks on their behalf. Acqueon’s advanced campaign manager was built “Compliance-first” since Day One and our Compliance strength is one of our platform’s core competitive differentiators.

Now, this new release takes the built-in Compliance capabilities to new, unmatched levels of value and ROI for your customers (especially those in highly regulated industries). Enhanced compliance capabilities now include adherence to critical Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) & Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) regulations.

DON’T SKIP THIS PART: We’ve released a new Compliance Condition Builder (CCB) that allows your customers to define rules on attempts across campaigns to seamlessly adhere to Collections requirements. CCB lets users cater to the 72-hour Ofcom rule.

Compliance Module Highlights:

Compliance Conditions Builder (CCB)

  • Create multiple compliance rules to check for attempts, type of voicemail, etc. across campaigns at the customer or account level

  • Control when a rule is to be executed for channels and modes

  • Apply rules across rolling days or hours

  • Create rules for number of attempts (for example, 7 attempts across 7 days)

  • Create rules for first or last attempt by checking the outcome or attempt time

  • Check for various types of attempts configured

  • Use three Unique IDs (such as Customer ID or Account Number) and phone numbers to track attempts

  • Use two system-level fields (like Due Date) in your rules

  • Check for Consent or Non-Consent

  • Control the rescheduling logic automatically, manually, or even close the contact

Customer Preferred Time (CPT)

  • Compliance Module checks for Customer Preferred time before dialing a customer

  • Up to 3 slots of Customer Preferred Time can be uploaded; by Day of Week, specific channels, and for specific campaigns and campaign categories

Global Business Parameters

  • The Global Business Parameters feature: additional 5 business parameters to be used at the application level

  • 3 Unique IDs – customer-related data such as Account ID and Customer ID

  • 2 Common Fields – for business-related data such as Due Days
