Acqueon AiQ™ Real-Time Guidance, Coaching & Live QM & the Impact on Average Handle Time (AHT)

Average Handle Time is a common call center metric that impacts KPIs from customer satisfaction to operational efficiency and agent effectiveness. It provides insight into the value of employee training programs, standard processes, and resources. AHT also plays a defining role in improving the customer experience. Simply put, AHT indicates an agent’s preparedness for handling customer interactions.

If an agent’s AHT is too high, it may indicate that:

  1. They don’t have adequate customer service experience or complete product knowledge

  2. Customers may find themselves calling multiple times to resolve a single issue

  3. The agent is not taking time to review calls or ensure they understand organizational processes

When your customers can reduce their Average Handle Time without sacrificing quality, their contact centers can make/take more calls in a day. And to reduce AHT, top-performing enterprise contact centers have flocked to AI-powered technology that enables amazing conversations while also reducing AHT.

Here's how Acqueon helps customers like yours reduce handle times:

Real-Time Guidance

Real-Time Guidance allows managers to monitor conversations while they’re ongoing, so issues can be identified and dealt with immediately, instead of post-call. It listens to both sides of the conversation and interprets the full context. Agent-facing insights enable agents to say the right thing at the right time, for a successful outcome every time.

After the conversation, the outcome data is fed back to Acqueon AiQ™ so wins can be scaled to other agents. Where Real-Time Agent Assist listens to keywords and focuses on providing recommendations for specific events, Real-Time Guidance considers the full context of each conversation.

Additionally, Real-Time Guidance allows agents to test different variations of responses to customers to make sure each outcome is as effective as possible. This information, like outcome data, is then relayed to managers and supervisors so they can identify successful call tracks and help agents who need it – all in real time.

Increase conversion rates, deliver exceptional service, boost agent confidence & stay compliant with Real-Time Guidance.

Real-Time Coaching

Acqueon AiQ™ listens to every call and automatically alerts managers of on-the-spot coaching opportunities. These real-time alerts enable managers to provide instant feedback to improve agent performance while saving hours of call monitoring time. The software can be programmed to pick up on keywords and phrases, so managers can jump in when needed and ensure that the appropriate response is provided every time. This reduces the need to escalate calls while providing a better customer experience.

Live QM

Say goodbye to random call sampling and listening to month-old call recordings. Acqueon’s Live QM leverages customizable scorecards to automatically grade every single call. Managers can stop focusing on listening to recordings and grading calls and, instead, focus on helping agents make every conversation their best conversation—and in much less time.
