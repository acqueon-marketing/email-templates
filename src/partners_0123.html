Banking Spotlight: Improving Collections & Compliance Using Acqueon

With 2022's 9.1% inflation rate, widespread layoffs, and a burgeoning recession, rising credit card delinquency rates are no shock to any of us. But for your banking customers? These issues are a monumental concern.

Acqueon helps the largest banks in the world solve unique customer engagement needs—credit card & mortgage collections are high on the list. Our top banking customers include U.S. Bank, Marcus by Goldman Sachs, Wells Fargo & IFDC India.

These are their most common/most urgent customer engagement concerns:

Rigid Processes Inhibit Segmentation

Legacy processes rarely account for organizational & customer evolution (ex- changes in a customer's earning patterns). Acqueon’s enables advanced contact segmentation and prioritization so banks make the most of precious agent resources & time.

No 360-View = Can’t Meet Customers’ 360-Needs

Collections agents often lack a complete customer view on their screen, making it impossible to offer the right payment options or guidance. When agents can provide personalized options, it fosters trust & empathy versus contention. Plus, agents have a higher likelihood of collecting overdue payments!

Complex Compliance & Costly Violations

Hassle and expense of maintaining Compliance are often our customers’ catalyst for seeking Acqueon's help. Acqueon’s advanced campaign manager lets you implement controls, run workflows on your data, and provide reports that mitigate risks while minimizing Compliance-related overhead costs.

No Control Over Collections Processes

Contact center or Collections teams often lack the inability to automate or adjust changes to the contact strategy. They lack agility or can't change what outreach channels are used. Acqueon enables the creation of multi-touch, omnichannel engagement sequences, including retries and next best actions.

Healthcare Spotlight: Patient & Member Engagement

6/10 Top Healthcare Payors & 20+ Major Health Systems Trust Acqueon. Here’s Why:

People expect to communicate with healthcare providers and payors via texts & automated voicemails about everything— prescriptions, refills, appointments, rescheduling & triage. Acqueon works with nearly three dozen hospitals, hospital systems and health insurance companies to solve their patient and member engagement needs. The result is amazing experiences for every patient, every member, every time.

Here’s a 50K foot snapshot of what we can do for your Healthcare customers:

Proactively Manage the Entire Patient Journey

Acqueon’s advanced outbound Campaign Manager is a complete omnichannel solution that integrates with the customer’s existing contact center and electronic health records (EHR) system. Your healthcare customers get to leverage the technology they’ve already made, plus, we deploy fast so they can start generating better outcomes & returns from that investment.

Transform Patient Outcomes with Seamless Epic EHR Access

Our platform-agnostic Epic EHR connector brings four core Epic workflows into the agent’s contact center desktop: Scheduling, Referral, Pharmacy & Triage workflows. Agents get a 360-view of the complete patient journey and medical history, along with easy to use workflows for scheduling/rescheduling appointments, converting referrals to appointments, filling prescriptions & triage with a clinician.

Launch Omnichannel Campaigns to Improve Health Outcomes & Revenue

Give your healthcare accounts the power & cost-efficiency of automating omnichannel engagement: live and automated campaigns are easily deployed on their customers' preferred channel/s: voice, SMS/text, email (and self-service).
