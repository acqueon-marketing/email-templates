Enterprise Connect Panel Recap: Why Every CX Leader Should be Stepping Up their Proactive Outreach Game?

Mike Harakal, Head of Revenue Execution Solutions

Imagine a world where every customer interaction, inbound or outbound, is a revenue-generating opportunity. That's the future unveiled by Robin Gareiss, Principal Analyst at Metrigy at Enterprise Connect. It’s a future Acqueon is uniquely positioned to deliver. Acqueon’s head of revenue execution solutions, Mike Harakal, spoke with Gareiss and industry peers about "Why Every CX Leader Should be Stepping Up their Proactive Outreach Game".

Beyond Proactive: A Revenue Execution Platform for the Omnichannel Era

Robin, Mike, and the panel dissected what proactive engagement truly means in today's complex contact center landscape. It's not enough to simply initiate contact. It's about executing revenue through personalized, timely, and compliant engagements across every communication channel across the customer journey.

Proactive tactics must extend beyond just outreach. They're equally vital tools for empowering agents (think real-time guidance, contextual information, and next-best-action suggestions). This is precisely the role of Acqueon's Revenue Execution Platform.

Here's how we help contact centers convert “proactive” to profit and revenue impact:

  • 💲 Capitalizing on Inbound Interest Mike posed the question, “How can I be better at getting to more of the inbound interest that might be coming towards me?” Acqueon’s Revenue Execution Platform enables your organization to seamlessly transition digital engagement into personalized voice, SMS, or email interactions, all within a unified workflow. This means you can proactively engage with every lead, turning "interest" into revenue.
  • 🤝 Empowering Agents for Revenue Generation The panel also emphasized the need to equip agents with the context and insights they need to anticipate customer needs and identify upselling opportunities. Acqueon does just that, turning every interaction into a potential revenue-generating moment.
  • 📊 Unifying Data & AI Across Channels Lastly, the session underscored the challenge of fragmented experiences. Acqueon addresses this by unifying data and AI, ensuring consistent and personalized experiences across all channels. We remove the silos that prevent great customer experiences and impede revenue growth.

Metrics That Matter: Right Party Contacts & Agent Productivity

As Robin Gareiss explained at Enterprise Connect 2025, "Ultimately, we're talking about conversions, whether it's reducing support issues or driving growth in sales."

Here are the two key metrics directly impacted by Acqueon:

  • Right Party Contacts (RPCs): We optimize outreach to connect with the right customers at the right time.
  • Agent Productivity: We empower agents with the tools and insights they need to close more deals and resolve issues faster.
