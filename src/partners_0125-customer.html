For Cisco Webex Contact Center (WxCC) Customers: Introducing Acqueon Patient Assist 🩺

Acqueon Patient Assist for Cisco Webex Contact Center is here! This integration empowers healthcare providers to deliver exceptional patient experiences while streamlining their operations.

Acqueon Patient Assist combines proactive engagement, our data platform and integrations, and our agent/clinician workspace with the power of the Cisco Webex Contact Center platform to provide a seamless and personalized patient journey that addresses critical use cases:

📆 Streamlined Patient Engagement: Patient Assist seamlessly integrates with Epic and Salesforce, reducing administrative overhead and enabling easy appointment scheduling and management for both patients and providers.

📲 Enhanced Patient Self-Service: Providers have flexible communication options, such as IVR, chatbot, and IVA, catering to patient preferences and enabling convenient self-service for appointment scheduling, prescription refills, and callbacks.

🩺 Efficient Post-Care Management: Acqueon Patient Assist simplifies follow-up scheduling with automated reminders and streamlines billing and collections processes with automated campaigns.

⚖️ Built-In Compliance: We know the importance of compliance and privacy. Acqueon Patient Assist never stores data and ensures that all patient outreach complies with federal and state regulations.
