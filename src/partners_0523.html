Acqueon Industry Insight: Contact Center Challenges & Pain Points

Fresh off the heels of our 2nd Annual Customer Advisory Board (CAB) Summit, we bring you an Acqueon industry insight. At CAB, team Acqueon was joined by 20 CX leaders from 15 enterprise customers for a day of interactive discussions, guest speakers, and Product Roadmap strategy sessions.

These conversations covered a wide range of topics, but they all revolved around a common theme – the top challenges and pain points everyone faces:

  1. Right Party Contact: "How can we actually reach the person we need to engage (for Sales/RevGen & Collections purposes)?"

  2. Agent Productivity: "Once our associates/employees are engaged with the right person, how do we make sure the interaction is maximized for revenue outcomes?"

Other notable challenges we heard from customers:
 

Typical Customer Results:
 

Ramp Time

Onboarding new agents takes a lot of time

Call Quality

It's difficult for agents to quickly find information to effectively address questions, leading to long wait times and bad customer experiences.

Manual Note Taking

Agents spend 1/3 of total call time on post-call actions (avg 3-5 mins spent summarizing notes).

Sales

Reps need access to proven rebuttals and responses during make-or-break moments.

Coaching

Managers don't have a way to help agents during their calls or identify coachable moments.

Compliance

Legal phrases and disclaimers that must be read verbatim are sometimes read incorrectly or missed, putting your company at risk

Acting on Insignts

You usually know what works best for your agents but lack a way to scale those learnings across your whole team

QA

Listining to calls and filling out forms for QA today takes a significant amount of time from the team leads.

Integration Risk & Friction

Many projects fail at the point on technology. We ensure that integration with your specific contact center platform is smooth, and implementation is complete in days.
