9 Contact Center Challenges Customers are Facing
 

Ramp Time

Onboarding new agents takes a lot of time.

Call Quality

It's difficult for agents to quickly find information to effectively address questions, leading to long wait times and bad customer experiences.

Manual Note Taking

Agents spend 1/3 of total call time on post-call actions (avg 3-5 mins spent summarizing notes).

Sales

Reps need access to proven rebuttals and responses during make-or-break moments.

Coaching

Managers don't have a way to help agents during their calls or identify coachable moments.

Compliance

Legal phrases and disclaimers that must be read verbatim are sometimes read incorrectly or missed, putting your company at risk.

Acting on Insignts

You usually know what works best for your agents but lack a way to scale those learnings across your whole team.

QA

Listining to calls and filling out forms for QA today takes a significant amount of time from the team leads.

Integration Risk & Friction
