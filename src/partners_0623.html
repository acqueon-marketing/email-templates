Enhanced Compliance! Upgraded Green Zone/Safe Harbor in Campaign Manager

Outbound Calling Compliance remains a top concern & core priority for every contact center. Enhanced Green Zone ensures dialing eligibility.

Contacts are pushed to the dialer ONLY if dialing safe zone is determined for time zones of all uploaded numbers for the contact vs. JUST the one being dialed.

This ensures FDCPA (Regulation F) compliance, which states all available information should be used to determine the best & most appropriate time to call.

Example: If a contact has three calling numbers, Acqueon Campaign Manager checks all 3 to ensure:

a) Time zones for all 3 numbers fall within the acceptable green zone or Safe Harbor parameters, and

b) Time zones for all mapped zip codes fall within Green Zone/Safe Harbor

The Enhanced Green Zone feature also checks timing rules against individual States determined from each area code & zip code.
