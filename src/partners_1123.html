Multi-region Redundancy Support

Your customers who rely on Acqueon Workspace for inbound screen pops, call control, and CRM access require minimal disruption in the event of a regional outage. That's why Acqueon Workspace now offers redundancy support across multiple AWS regions. This capability replicates desktop configuration data across AWS regions with full backups and configuration snapshots enabled.
